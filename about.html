About Us

We believe in people--especially family and friends--and more often than not, we want to spend our time with people alongside good food and drinks. We've taken that belief and laid it as our groundwork when we opened Sprove Marketplace in 2017. Family owned and friendly. We'll focus on the food and drinks at the table so you can continue on in conversation and laughter.

Come by and find multiple meal options from salads, sushi, juices, sandwiches, stir fry and a daily changing hot and cold bar. Of course, you can also pick up your standard groceries or swing over to the corner for some beer, wine and spirits. If you don't mind staying a little long, we have seats and tables upstairs.
